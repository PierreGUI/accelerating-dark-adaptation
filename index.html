Luminous Emittance

Lux is the unit used to measure the luminous emittance, as perceived by the human eye.
A moonless overcast night sky has a lux of 0.0001 and an office lighting ranges from 320 to 500 lux.

About sky quality

Sky quality measures the luminance of the night sky. Amateur astronomers use it to quantify the skyglow aspect of light pollution.
An illuminated road has a sky quality lower than 16 and a clear night sky is higher than 20.
